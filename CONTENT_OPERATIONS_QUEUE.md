## Workflow Rules
1. **Focus on Freshness**: Always ensure top merchants have updated offers before writing new blog content.
2. **Cluster Content**: If you add a new merchant, immediately queue up related FAQ and blog content.
3. **Data Velocity**: Set a weekly quota for new coupon imports (e.g., +500 coupons/week) and track it here.
